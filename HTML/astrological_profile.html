for the rest of the year; plus, a few retrogrades are finally bowing out, thank God). To capitalize on this momentous time for literally everyone, we put Jake Register of Jake’s Astrology to work
with our fashion team to find your luckiest look for your peak fall moment.

We’ve just about hit the midsummer slump: that time
every year when our new, warm-weather pieces just don’t seem
as fun as they did when we first took them home from the store.
We still love our off-the-shoulder tops, choker
necklaces, cutoff shorts, and collection of bomber jackets,
but we also yearn for new ways to style our old favorites.
A suggestion? Look to the stars. Your next summer go-to
may already be written in the sky. Scroll through the
slideshow for 30 midsummer outfit ideas based on your sign.
From the risk-taking Leo to the elegant Virgo,
we've got your summer style horoscope covered.

AQUARIUS



You’re creative and original, marching to the beat of your own drum.
While you have an eye for fashion and know all about the biggest trends
of the season, they mean nothing to you when it comes to what you choose to wear.
You know what you like and rock all your favorite pieces like few others can.



Keep it simple with jean shorts, but add fun details like a
sweatshirt around your waist or a pair of killer heels.



CANCER


You know what you like, and you stick to it. That doesn’t mean you
gravitate toward boring pieces, though. After all, classics are just
our favorite essentials, and they’re anything but stuffy. When packing
your bag for a summer getaway, you can’t imagine leaving your wardrobe
staples behind: vintage tees, denim shorts, and low-top sneakers all the way.



Bring back the denim mini this summer (and straight into fall)
by pairing it with a tank and ankle booties.



LIO


You’re independent and self-sufficient…until you’re not.
You’re the girl who busts out of the house in a head-turning
look, guns blazing, but needs a little bit of an audience once
you’re outside. (Hey…we’re not judging! Everyone loves to be
told how awesome they look from time to time!) You keep your look
classic but make sure to have a totally bold piece or
two to make people stare


Sometimes it's as simple as a bright pop of color in a bag or accessory.


Pull out all the stops when it comes to your jumpsuit: Try
sheer details, a statement bow, and accessorize with strappy heels.



VIRGO


You’re powerful but indecisive at times. While you may need
a little push to convince you that you're on the best path,
you know—deep down—you were right all along.
Your fashion choices mirror your temperament as you
keep it simple, but look for details that make you smile.


Add a little flair with a ruffle or off-the-shoulder
style instead of reaching for traditional shirting.


Let your top do the talking, amping up your go-to
skinny-jeans look with a bit of flair.



LIBRA


You’re a little laid-back from time to time, but never when
it comes to your fashion. While you may have dreams of a bright
future, the journey to get there seems so long. In the meantime?
You look the part: always pulled together and always dressed to the nines.


Layer with the classics, like a leather or denim jacket


Channel Grace Kelly in a tea-length strapless dress for an evening event



SCORPIO


You’re a true Queen Bey: an independent woman who not only
knows she is but seriously flaunts it. You don’t need the
craziest trend or boldest statement accessory to show the
world who’s boss. You’re set with an LBD and a pair of killer heels.


Go for that ever-trendy '90s style with a simple slip dress and ankle booties.



SAGITTARIUS


You’re more adventurous than anyone you know, but totally
easygoing no matter what situation comes your way. Your look
reflects your carefree attitude: bright, bold, and always comfortable.


Add a pair of whimsical heels to an elevated athleisure look.



CAPRICORNN


You’re independent, but in a way that leads you to support
those around you. You’re always put-together; you make heads
turn and friends wonder how they can cop your impeccable style.
As such, your wardrobe is filled to the brim with the classics:
ankle-cropped denim, crisp white shirting, loafers, the works.


Go for a long-line coat in a bright color to top off your jeans and T-shirt look.


Modernize your lace outfit by opting for a slip dress and accessorizing
with chunky-heeled mules.



PISCES


You’re a little bit of a dreamer, often finding your head stuck
in the clouds. No matter: It’s a great place to be when it
comes to fashion! Your style is wood nymph meets hippie meets
effervescent fashionista. If only you were born in the ‘60s


Show a little skin with your boho outfit by opting for a crop
top and high-waisted style.



ARIES


You’re a go-getter and totally independent, which translates
into a try-anything attitude when it comes to your clothing.
You’re the first one to wear even the craziest of runway trends
IRL, and you know you look awesome doing it. What’s fashion if not fun?



TARUS


You’re independent, but never the first one out of the gate
when it comes to venturing into the great unknown.You may wait
until midseason to jump into any of the newest trends,
but once you find them, too much is never enough.


Try out the sheer trend, but keep it refined with a longer, high-neck silhouette.



GIMINI


You can’t imagine not exercising your God-given right to be
your own person. You're extremely independent but also quite
stubborn — there’s no question you make everything in your
wardrobe your own. You mix and match trends with fervor and
thoroughly enjoy every moment of the reckless abandonment with which you get dressed


Mix prints and colors in tailored silhouettes for a fun yet elegant look.
