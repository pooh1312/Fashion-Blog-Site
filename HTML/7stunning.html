7 Stunning Pride Make-up Ideas to Inspire Your 2020 Look

"Show them off with- you guessed it-pride. "



The only rule when it comes to Pride make-up? There are no rules. It’sall about
illustrating joy, individuality, and love—with a touch of glitter.We collected
7 statement-making ideas from the LGBTQ+ community and allies alike to inspire you
for Pride2020,whether you’re looking for full on rainbow motifs,flashes of sparkle
or something more subtly symbol








Allover Sparkle

Never one to shy away from a theme , BillyPorter
paired his custom outfit from The Blonds with face
jewels,silver eyeliner,and deep purple lipstick at London Pride in 2019.



















Glitter Unicorn



pink, purple,and Pride: Josephine Skriver’s imaginative look
at the 2019 New York City World Pride Parade was a
departure from the sea of rainbow,and proved that
anything goes.
















Applying pearls with eyelash glue and adding faux
“sparkle” with white liner gives rainbow shadow even
more dimension.














Half Rainbow



Singer Lauren Jauregui wore cool tones on one lid and
warm tones on the other for her performance at the 2019
New York City World Pride Parade.




















Rainbow Lashes



Rainbow lashes are gorgeous on their own—or if
you're feeling extra,paired with any of the other
looks on this list.If you can't track down every
shade of mascara,you'll get a similar effect with
three or four colors.
















It Takes Two



James Charles and Kesha’s (nearly) twinning
looks give you double the multi-color options.The
questionis:will you choose matte or glitter?













Love Is Life



Victoria Justice let her outfit do most of the talking
at the 2019 New York City World Pride Parade, but
added a small heart decal on her cheek and bold
red lip to complete her look.
